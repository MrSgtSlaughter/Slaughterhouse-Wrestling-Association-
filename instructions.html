HOW THE GAME WORKS
Wrestling Story is a career simulation game. Each playthrough lasts 25 weeks.
1
CHOOSE A WRESTLER — Each wrestler has unique stats and a special finisher move. Enter your name for the leaderboard.
2
FLIP A CHANCE CARD — At the start of every week, you draw a Monopoly-style chance card. Life events happen — good AND bad. Your stats can go up or down!
3
ANSWER A QUESTION — Every week you face an Ancient China question. Answer correctly to raise your INTELLIGENCE stat by +1.
4
PICK A MATCH OR TRAIN — Choose from 3 opponents (or a title match!), or skip the match to train a stat. Training adds +1 to any stat of your choice.
5
FIGHT! — Turn-based battles using dice rolls + your stats. Pick the right moves. Win your match to earn money and XP.
6
SPEND MONEY — Visit the Shop between matches to buy stat boosts, hire a manager, upgrade your finisher, or clear an injury.
7
WIN TITLES — Rack up enough wins to qualify for title shots. Capture the Indie, Regional, National, and World titles.
8
SUBMIT YOUR SCORE — After 25 weeks, your career ends. Submit your score to the Hall of Fame leaderboard!
💡 THE BIG PICTURE Your final score is calculated from wins, title belts, money earned, level, and intelligence. The path to a high score = WIN often, EARN money, answer QUESTIONS correctly, and chase TITLES.
🗓
25-WEEK SEASON
Your career runs exactly 25 weeks. After that, your final record is tallied and you can submit your score. Plan your training weeks wisely — every week counts!
⭐
LEVELS & XP
You earn XP from every match — win or lose. When you level up, you choose +1 to any stat. Higher levels also make opponents stronger to keep things fair.
💾
SAVE & LOAD
Hit SAVE from the career hub to save to one of 3 slots. Load from the title screen. Your save includes everything — stats, money, titles, week number.
🏅
LEADERBOARD
Scores are stored locally. At game end, submit your score to the Hall of Fame. Click SCORES from the career hub anytime to see the rankings.
YOUR SIX STATS
Every wrestler has 6 stats, each from 1 to 20. Stats affect everything — how hard you hit, how often you land moves, and how the story unfolds.

⚡ SPEED Used to land STRIKE and HIGH RISK moves. Faster wrestlers dodge more. Can be hurt by injuries Strikers, high flyers

⚠ STATS CAN DECREASE!
Chance cards, injuries, and life events can permanently reduce your stats. A knee injury drops SPD and STA. A bad week on the road drops STA and CHA. Stats can never go below 1. Always check your card effects before dismissing them!

BATTLE SYSTEM
Every match is turn-based. You pick a move, the dice roll, then the enemy automatically takes their turn. Repeat until someone's HP hits zero.
🎲 HOW DICE ROLLS WORK Every move is a contest. Your roll = d20 + half your relevant stat. Enemy roll = d20 + half their stat. Whoever rolls higher wins the exchange. Roll 18-20 = CRITICAL HIT (1.5× damage)!

💥
CRITICAL HITS
If your attack roll is 18, 19, or 20, you land a CRITICAL HIT dealing 1.5× normal damage. High rolls are everything!
💀
FUMBLES
Power Slam and High Risk can fumble — you take self-damage instead of dealing it. Don't spam risky moves when your HP is low!
🤖
ENEMY AI
Enemies use their finisher when below 30% HP and try to heal when critically low. They also get stronger as your level increases.
📈
MOMENTUM
The yellow bar shows momentum. Landing hits gains momentum. Taking hits loses it. High momentum is a visual indicator that you're in control!
💡 STRATEGY BY WRESTLER TYPE
💪 HIGH STR (Bruiser, Raging Rhino): Spam Power Slam early, save your Finisher for when the enemy is under 40% HP.
🧠 HIGH TEC (Ring Blade, Iron Maiden): Grapple every turn — it uses TEC for both landing AND damage. Super consistent.
⚡ HIGH SPD (Blue Shadow, Violet Vixen): Strike often to build momentum. Use High Risk only when above 70% HP.
🌟 HIGH CHA (Midnight Mauler): Your Finisher deals massive damage. Survive long enough to use it, then clean up.
🔮 INT 10+ (any wrestler): Smart Counter is your secret weapon — uses INT to roll, TEC for damage, zero risk.

🔮 INTELLIGENCE — ANCIENT CHINA
Every week after your chance card, you face ONE question from the Ancient China unit. This is the only way to raise your INT stat.
✓
CORRECT ANSWER
Answer correctly and your INT increases by +1. You also see a full explanation of why that answer is correct.
✗
WRONG ANSWER
INT stays the same. The correct answer is revealed with an explanation so you can learn for next time.
🔮
WHAT INT DOES
INT 10+: Unlocks the SMART COUNTER move in battle (no risk, INT roll, TEC damage).

INT 15+: Gives +2 to ALL TEC rolls passively — your grapples become much more accurate.

⚠ INJURY SYSTEM
Losing a match has a 25% chance of giving you an injury penalty (reduced to 12% with Health Insurance). An injured wrestler fights with −1 effective STA (less max HP) in the next match. Buy Recovery Treatment from the Shop, or win your next match to shake it off.

SCORE = (WINS × 100) + (TITLES × 500) + (MONEY ÷ 100) + (LEVEL × 50) + (INT × 10)

💡 TIPS & STRATEGIES
Pro tips to maximize your score and career.
📚
STUDY BEFORE PLAYING
Questions come from your actual class notes. Review Confucianism, Daoism, Legalism, the three dynasties, and key figures. INT is worth 10 pts per level!
💾
SAVE OFTEN
Save after every title win and any time your stats improve significantly. You can't undo chance cards, but you CAN reload a save if things go badly.
🎯
CHASE TITLES FAST
Title matches pay 2-5× more than regular matches AND give 500 leaderboard points. Get your wins up quickly so title opportunities appear early.
🤵
BUY THE MANAGER EARLY
$3,000 upfront, but +15% on every purse after. If you win 15 more matches averaging $1,200 each, the manager earns you an extra $2,700. Worth it!
🔮
ANSWER EVERY QUESTION
INT is the only free stat (via questions). Every other stat costs $600 in the shop or a training week. Getting to INT 15 gives you passive battle bonuses.
⚡
USE YOUR FINISHER WISELY
The Finisher is one-use per match. Use it when the enemy is around 30-40% HP to guarantee the knockout. Don't waste it when the enemy is at full health.
❤️
RECOVER WHEN HURT
If you're below 40% HP, consider using Recover instead of attacking. A healed wrestler who survives longer beats a reckless wrestler every time.
🔥
RIVALRY MATCHES PAY MORE
Face the same opponent twice and your third fight becomes a Rivalry Match worth 2-3× normal pay AND bonus XP. Let a rivalry develop before settling the score!
🏋
TRAIN AT THE RIGHT TIME
Training is best when: your weakest stat is holding you back, you have plenty of weeks left, or a title shot needs one more stat point to be competitive.
